Roald Dahl

Roald Dahl was born on the 13th of Semtember 1916 in Llandaff, South Wales. You may be suprised to know that English was not his best subject at school yet somehow he wrote over 60 books 19 of which were childrens books. After he published the best-selling story collection(Someone like you) was published he married award winning actress, Patricia Neal in 1953. When she died in 1983 he married Felicity d"Abreu Crosland. Dahl spent over 20 years writing books and died of myelodysplastic syndrome on the 23rd of November 1990 while he was writing Charlie in the White House.
